About Us

H.H. Engineering PLC - Pioneering Ethiopian Industry

H.H. Engineering PLC is a leading Ethiopian company established with a strong vision to drive industrial growth through integrated engineering solutions. Founded on a solid capital base and guided by a legacy of innovation, the company specializes in manufacturing and assembling heavy trucks and trailers, constructing oil depots, producing steel structures, as well as import-export and general trading services.

These core business areas collectively support our mission to build a robust industrial foundation, create sustainable job opportunities, enhance local capacity, and make a lasting positive impact on both the community and the national economy.

Dedicated to advancing industrial growth in Ethiopia and beyond, we maintain a steadfast commitment to innovation, quality, and excellence, fully aligned with the country's goals of industrialization and self-reliance.
