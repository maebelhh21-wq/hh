About HH ENGINEERING

Founded with a vision to bridge Ethiopia with the global market, HH ENGINEERING has grown into a comprehensive trade and manufacturing company. We are proud to represent Ethiopia's rich agricultural heritage while simultaneously building its industrial future.

Our expertise spans from sourcing the finest Ethiopian coffee beans and sesame seeds from local farmers to ensuring they meet international quality standards for export. Simultaneously, we import essential construction materials and machinery that fuel Ethiopia's development.

Our heavy vehicle assembly plant combines international technology with local expertise, creating employment opportunities and reducing dependency on fully imported vehicles.

At HH ENGINEERING, we believe in sustainable growth, ethical business practices, and contributing to Ethiopia's economic transformation.
